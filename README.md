# Zoho-Expense-Tracking-Framework-
Advanced expense tracking system using Zoho Books, including categorization, owner reimbursement, and billable expense management.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The goal of this project was to:
- Record and categorize multiple travel-related expenses  
- Set up a proper liability account for reimbursements  
- Ensure visibility through dashboards and reports  
- Apply correct accounting treatment for billable expenses  

---

## 💰 Expense Summary

A total of **$1,444.70** was recorded across five expenses:

- **Delta Flight** – $450.00  
  Category: Travel  
  Description: Business trip flight  

- **Fairmont Austin Hotel** – $825.00  
  Category: Lodging  
  Description: Accommodation during the summit  

- **Terry Black’s BBQ** – $65.00  
  Category: Meals  
  Description: Business meal (**Billable to client**)  

- **Tech-Stop UK Gift** – $74.25  
  Category: Other Expenses  
  Description: Business-related purchase  

- **Mileage** – $30.45  
  Category: Fuel/Mileage  
  Description: 42 miles reimbursed  

---

## 🧾 Chart of Accounts Setup

A dedicated liability account was created to properly track reimbursements:

- **Account Name:** Due to Owner (Owner Reimbursement)  
- **Account Type:** Other Current Liability  
- **Structure:** Standalone account (not a sub-account)  
- **Purpose:** Tracks funds owed to Jim for out-of-pocket expenses  

This ensured accurate financial reporting and clear visibility on the Balance Sheet.

## ⚙️ Accounting Treatment

### Paid Through Logic
All expenses were assigned to:
- **Paid Through:** Due to Owner  

This ensures:
- No misclassification under petty cash  
- Proper tracking of reimbursement obligations  

### Billable Expense Handling
- $65 (Meals) marked as **Billable**  
- Linked to customer: Global Tech Corp  
- Ready for client invoicing and cost recovery  

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

- Importance of using liability accounts for owner-funded expenses  
- Proper categorization improves financial clarity and reporting  
- Billable expenses help recover costs and improve profitability  
- Dashboard customization enhances real-time decision-making
